YouTube Collage [beta]
LOGS

This application creates a collage of YouTube videos.

Currently, two services from where videos can be fetched are supported: Facebook and Youtube.

For YouTube, you need to use the username of the uploader and it will fetch the videos that were uploaded by that person, and for Facebook you need to use an entity's (Person, Group or Page) ID (which you can get from the URL) and it will fetch the videos that are posted on that entity's wall.

To load more videos, scroll to the bottom of the page or press 'Space'. If you're sick of doing that and want all the videos at once, press the 'Fetch all' button.
